Research Works

Conference Paper

6LoWPAN Based Futuristic Smart Architecture for Home Automation

The proposed system provided efficient connectivity, mobility, and scalability between a 6LoWPAN node and IP devices, eliminating the need for an external gateway. Additionally, the system extends a better operational lifetime to 79.70 days compared to other wireless automation models like Bluetooth and ZigBee. Besides, It demonstrates compatibility with various networks and ensures streamlined end-to-end communication with a 31% reduced delay. Moreover, the system facilitates easy expansion of integrating new sensor nodes into the existing network.
