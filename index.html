About Me

Researcher, Educator & Engineer

I'm Taufiqul Islam Khan, a Computer Science graduate researcher at the University of Manitoba, working under Professor Shaowei Wang at the MAMBA Lab. My research focuses on software engineering, code review automation, and leveraging large language models to build smarter development tools.

Before starting my M.Sc., I spent over three years as a full-time lecturer at BRAC University in Dhaka, where I taught Software Engineering, Programming, Computer Interfacing, and Robotics. I also served as Course Coordinator, designed curricula, and mentored students through their academic journeys.

On the industry side, I've worked as a software engineer building backend services with Spring Boot, data processing pipelines with Python and AWS, and machine learning models for real-world applications including license plate detection and data validation systems.

I'm driven by the intersection of teaching, research, and engineering — building tools that make software development more efficient while helping the next generation of developers grow.
