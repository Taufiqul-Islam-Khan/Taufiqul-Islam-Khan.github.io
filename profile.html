About Me

Lecturer, BRAC University

Currently, I hold the position of a lecturer at BRAC University, where I share my expertise in Computer Science and Engineering. I completed my Bachelor's degree in the same field at BRAC University, and after my undergraduate studies, I garnered over two years of valuable industry experience as a software engineer in renowned software companies.

At the time of my undergraduate study at BRAC University (2016-2020), I maintained an outstanding 3.9 CGPA, earning a place on both the VC’s and Dean’s List. Also, by Securing a merit-based scholarship, I consistently demonstrated academic excellence. Not only that, but I also performed well in my earlier education, including receiving the Higher Secondary Government Scholarship (82nd position) and Primary School Government Scholarship (17th position) for my exceptional performance. Moreover, as a Teaching Assistant in BRAC University (2019-2020), I supported professors in courses e.g. Algorithms and Digital Logic Design, handling various responsibilities for the department. Additionally, as a Student Mentor (2018-2019) at the Office of Academic Advising, I actively monitored and assessed students’ academic performance, providing guidance and consultation.
